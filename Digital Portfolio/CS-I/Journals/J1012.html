1.1: Describe the general process for executing addition, regardless of base.

Align the digits vertically, add the right-most column, if sum is greater than quanity that can be represented by a single digit- carry over, then if more to left repeat.

2.1: What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with octal numbers?

The quantity range is 0-7, if exceeds minus number by 8 then carry a 1; 8 is like your 10.

3.1: What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with hexadecimal numbers?

The quantity range is 0-9 and A-F/10-15, if exceeds minus number by 16 then carry a 1; 16 is like your 10. But also 10-15 is represented by letters.

4.1: What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with binary numbers?

The quantity range is 0-1, if exceeds minus number by 2 then carry; 2 is like your 10.
